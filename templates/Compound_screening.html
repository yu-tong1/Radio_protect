About the system

Core functions

  • Match TCM compounds against radiation‑related pathways
  • Map compound CID to pathway details (type, targets, gene IDs)
  • Export matched & unmatched results as CSV (UTF‑8)
  • Support for normal model（X‑ray, γ‑rays, α‑particles, heavy metals）and cancer model（X‑ray, γ‑rays,protons）

Technical background

  • Python backend (pandas) with precomputed pathway mapping
  • KEGG pathway IDs and gene targets integration
  • Prediction rates from molecular docking / ML models
  • Traditional Chinese Medicine ingredients database

How to use

  1. Prepare your file CSV with at least one column: compound CID (e.g., 7937).
  2. Upload & run Click “Run screening”. The system simulates matching against pre‑loaded pathway data.
  3. Explore results Two tables show matched (with full pathway details) and unmatched compounds.
  4. Download Use the CSV buttons to get exactly the same files as produced by Compound screening.py (save_file).

The demo button loads a representative dataset to illustrate full output.
